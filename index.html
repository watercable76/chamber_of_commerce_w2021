Description of the purpose of the chamber and the goals The local ACM chapter here in Rexburg is designed to better prepare students to start working in industry. We are actively working with students and faculty to accommodate for the needs of students and are always trying to do better. One of the many things we are working on is the Acorn Project. One of the most desired skills is professional work experience, and it is hard to get that without having an actual job. This project allows for students, faculty and departments to host projects and 'hire' students. These students can then work on projects and earn 'money', or acorns. These acorns can then be used to pay others to work on projects, essentially creating more jobs for even more students.



Idaho News

Idaho is such a diverse place, rich with forests, a variety of outdoor activities, large open areas and beautiful sunsets. All of these events are available in each part of the state, and some parts of the state offer all of these activities.


Some great places to visit include Fish Haven, Soda Springs and Preston. All of these cities are very friendly and have so much to do! If you would like to know more about all of these places, visit the links above, which will bring you to a site with more information.
