Come join us as we prepare you for getting a job out of college and enjoying your time here in Rexburg! We are a dedicated student led society that focuses on real world skills and preparing people for working in industry.

The local ACM chapter here in Rexburg is designed to better prepare students to start working in industry. We are actively working with students and faculty to accommodate for the needs of students and are always trying to do better.

One of the many things we are working on is the Acorn Project. One of the most desired skills is professional work experience, and it is hard to get that without having an actual job. This project allows for students, faculty and departments to host projects and 'hire' students. These students can then work on projects and earn 'money', or acorns. These acorns can then be used to pay others to work on projects, essentially creating more jobs for even more students.

We hope you join us as we navigate your life here in Rexburg and start building your future with us.
