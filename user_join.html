Join Us

Each of these listed above are memberships options for the local chamber of commerce. With the non-profit membership, this donation funds the local events like hackathons, food in the park, and even events like the 4th of July celebration.


The bronze membership includes a discount to select businesses in the town. This includes Jimmy Johns, Gator Jacks and Subway.


The silver membership includes even more discounts! Every time you go to the fairgrounds, you will get a 30% discount from all of your purchases at the food stand. Also, kids under 5 will get into the rodeo with you for free.


The gold membership is the important one. This includes all of the previously mentioned discounts and so much more! People who subscribe for this will qualify for the opportunity to be on the chamber of commerce's board of directors. The members of the board of directors help listen to the community and determine new legislature that will be passed in order to better help the community we are all part of.
