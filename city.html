Summer is Here

With the recent global warming, the world has taken a turn for the worse. Instead of insane winters that seem to freeze people as they take their morning walks, we are faced with a large deficit of snow and cold. This is highly unusual, as this promotes more ice build up instead of beautiful snow.

The warm weather has melted a lot of the snow in the area, but this does not mean winter is over. We are still seeing some patches of snow, ice and freezing mornings still exist.
